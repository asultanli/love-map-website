Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

LoveMap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the LoveMap mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and name when you create an account.
  • Assessment data: Your responses to our relationship assessment questions, which are used to compute your personality and compatibility profile.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app, including screens visited and features used, collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the LoveMap experience
  • Compute your relationship profile and compatibility report
  • Generate personalized daily tips and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data only with:

  • Your partner: When you link accounts, your compatibility profile is shared with your linked partner within the app.
  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider, used to store your account and profile data securely.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for anonymous analytics to understand app usage patterns.
  • Apple: When you use Sign in with Apple, Apple processes your authentication.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption. Assessment data is stored in our database and protected by row-level security policies that ensure you can only access your own data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at sultanliaykhan@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

LoveMap is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 17 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
